Hans Zimmer



Hans Florian Zimmer (German pronunciation: [ˈhans ˈfloːʁi̯aːn ˈtsɪmɐ] (About this soundlisten); born 12 September 1957) is a German film score composer and record producer.

Since the 1980s, Zimmer has composed music for over 150 films. His works include:
  • The Lion King
  • Pirates of the Caribbean
  • The Dark Knight Trilogy
  • Inception
  • Blade Runner 2049
